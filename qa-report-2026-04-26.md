### IntegrationTests — infra failure ⚠️

All 137 DB-backed integration tests fail with:

```

(Parameter 'DockerEndpointAuthConfig')
```

**Root cause:** `/var/run/docker.sock` exists but is owned by `root:root` (`srw-rw----`).
The `agent` user (uid=1001) is not in the `docker` group and cannot open the socket.
